LAUREN

HOM

"Work Hard, Snack Often"

Lauren Hom is a designer, illustrator, and hand lettering artist based in Detroit, Michigan. Hom picked up hand lettering as a hobby and was catapulted from the world of passion projects to a thriving freelance business. Besides taking on her client projects, Hom enjoys teaching others how to find success for themselves through workshops, online courses, and her blog.

The quote, "Work Hard, Snack Often", is Hom's personal motto. She explains the importance of taking breaks and maintaining a healthy balance between work and personal time. As stated, "I realize that working hard all the time isn't sustainable". This quote works as a reminder to all people and designers to take time for yourself.
