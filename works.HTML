WORKS

Hom's expertise in hand lettering has attracted clients far and wide. From Starbucks to Target, you have likely seen her work and not known it. When she's not taking on client work, Hom hosts workshops to help others interested in hand lettering, design, and freelancing. She has been an inspiration to many and will continue to become a household name through her works.
